Small amount loans in Colombia

Last updated: 2026-02-01

Information about small loans and microloans in Colombia. Options for borrowing small amounts between $100,000 and $1,000,000 COP.

Overview of Small Loans

Small amount loans typically range from $100,000 to $1,000,000 pesos and are designed for minor expenses or emergencies. They're often easier to qualify for than larger loans.

Typical characteristics

  • Amounts: $100,000 - $1,000,000 COP
  • Terms: 7 days to 3 months
  • Quick approval process
  • Minimal documentation requirements
  • Higher interest rates

Best for

Small loans work well for:

  • Emergency expenses
  • Building first credit history
  • Quick cash needs
  • Testing a lender before larger loans

Important considerations

  • Calculate true cost including all fees
  • Ensure repayment capacity
  • Read complete terms
  • Verify lender legitimacy
