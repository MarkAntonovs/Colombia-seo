About this site

This website provides informative guidance about loans and financial services in Colombia. We are not a financial entity or credit intermediaries.

Purpose of the content

Our goal is to help people make more informed financial decisions by providing guides on requirements, risks, and how to identify potential fraud related to online loans.

Important

We do not process loans, do not offer financial services, and do not request money for any reason. If someone contacts you on our behalf requesting payments or banking information, it is fraud.
