What does "instant" mean in online loans?

Many online loan platforms advertise "instant", "immediate" or "in minutes" loans. It's important to understand what these terms really mean.

Reality of "instant"

"Instant" generally refers to:

  • Quick response: Approval or rejection communicated in minutes
  • Digital process: Everything done online without in-person visits
  • Same day: At best, money same day as application

What it does NOT mean

  • Money in your account in literally 5 minutes
  • Without any verification or process
  • Guaranteed approval for everyone

Real process times

These are the most realistic timeframes in Colombia for 2026:

  • Initial evaluation: 5-30 minutes
  • Identity verification: 10-60 minutes
  • Credit analysis: 30 minutes - 2 hours
  • Approval: Immediate after analysis
  • Disbursement: 1 hour - 24 hours

Realistic total: Best case: 2-4 hours. Typical: 4-24 hours. Special cases: Up to 48 hours.

Factors affecting speed

1. Application timing

Applications during banking hours (Monday-Friday, 8am-5pm) process faster. Night or weekend applications may delay until next business day.

2. Your credit profile

Good history profiles process faster. If you have negative reports in Datacrédito or incomplete information, it may take longer.

3. New vs returning customer

Returning customers with positive payment history have faster processes. Applications without prior history require more verification.

4. Disbursement method

Transfers to traditional bank accounts are usually faster than to digital wallets.

5. Amount requested

Small amounts generally approve faster than large amounts requiring more analysis.

Step-by-step process

To get a loan as quickly as possible:

  1. Prepare your documentation (ID, income info, bank details)
  2. Verify specific requirements before applying
  3. Complete application accurately
  4. Respond quickly to verification requests
  5. Stay available for phone verification
  6. Review terms before accepting

Warnings and warning signs

Signs of possible scams:

  • "Money in 5 minutes guaranteed without verification"
  • "100% approval guaranteed for everyone"
  • Request for advance payment to "release" the loan
  • Excessive pressure to decide immediately
  • Lack of clear information about rates and conditions

Before giving personal data, confirm the entity is legal. Check how to verify if a lending company is legitimate.

Frequently asked questions

Can I receive money in less than an hour?

Uncommon but not impossible for returning customers with good history, small amounts, and during banking hours. For new customers, 4-24 hours is more realistic.

Are instant loans more expensive?

Generally yes. Convenience of speed usually translates to higher interest rates and shorter terms. Learn about interest rates.

What if I need money in the next few hours?

For extreme emergencies, first consider interest-free options: payroll advance, family support, or negotiation with whom you need to pay. "Instant" loans are rarely that instant.
